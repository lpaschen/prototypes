How Self Benchmarks Work

Self Benchmarks compare your current performance to your own historical data — not industry averages. This makes the signals more meaningful and personalized to your audience.

📐 Baseline method

We use a rolling median of your prior 8 completed weeks (excluding the current week). The median reduces the impact of outlier weeks so you get a stable, noise-resistant signal.

🔔 When do I get an insight?

An insight surfaces when your weekly total is more than 20% above or below the baseline — a threshold tuned to reduce noise and surface only meaningful changes.

📊 What counts as an engagement?

Clicks on shortened links + QR code scans, combined.
